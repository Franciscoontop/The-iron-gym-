Our Story

We Built This
For Workers.

"I was a competitive powerlifter who hated big-box gyms with too many rules and not enough chalk — so I built this."

Marcus "Tank" Reed opened The Iron Den six years ago in an old warehouse in Port Morris. The mission was simple: create a space where serious athletes could train heavy, lift loud, and get stronger without being judged.

Today, The Iron Den is home to blue-collar workers, first responders, and competitive athletes from across the Bronx and beyond. No cardio theater. No influencers. Just work.

Our proudest moment? Watching a client who couldn't walk up stairs three years ago recently pull a 300 lb deadlift.

Quick Answers

Common
Questions.

Is the gym really open 24 hours? 01

Yes. Members get 24/7 key-fob access — no staffed attendant required. Staffed hours for tours and new sign-ups are Mon–Fri, 4 PM–8 PM.

Do you allow chalk? 02

Absolutely. We're a black-iron gym — chalk isn't just allowed, it's encouraged. We have it available for members on the floor.

Is there a contract? 03

No contracts. The Grinder membership is month-to-month. Cancel anytime. We keep you because of results, not fine print.

Can I drop weights? 04

Yes. This is a real gym. Drop it if you earned it. We have proper rubber flooring and a crew that won't give you side-eyes.

Do you have showers? 05

Come in for a tour during staffed hours (Mon–Fri 4–8 PM) and we'll walk you through the full facility including locker room amenities.

How do I book a tour? 06

Two ways: fill out the contact form below, or text the word POWER to 718-555-9012. We'll get you in during staffed hours.
